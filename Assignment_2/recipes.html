DIY Beauty Recipes

Coconut Lip Balm

Ingredients: 2 tbsp coconut oil, 1 tbsp beeswax, 5 drops essential oil.

Steps: Melt coconut oil and beeswax, mix in essential oil, pour into a container, and cool.

Oatmeal Face Mask

Ingredients: 2 tbsp oatmeal, 1 tbsp honey, 1 tsp water.

Steps: Mix ingredients into a paste, apply to face for 15 minutes, rinse off.
