Eco-Friendly Beauty Tips

Reuse Containers: Clean and reuse glass jars for your DIY products instead of buying new ones.

Buy in Bulk: Purchase ingredients like coconut oil or beeswax in bulk to reduce packaging waste.

Avoid Synthetics: Stick to natural ingredients to keep your beauty routine biodegradable.
