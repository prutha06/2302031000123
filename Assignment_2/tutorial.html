DIY Lavender Lip Balm

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp shea butter
  • 1 tbsp beeswax
  • 5 drops lavender essential oil

Instructions

  1. Melt shea butter and beeswax in a double boiler over low heat.
  2. Stir until fully blended.
  3. Remove from heat, add lavender oil, and mix well.
  4. Pour into a small tin or jar and let it solidify for 2-3 hours.
